Market Calculator

  • Provide the information on your desired card.
  • Generate both a buy order and a sell order to flip a card.
    • Target buy and target sell will be a spread from the midpoint of the current spread.
    • Make sure your desired margin is smaller than the current spread.
    • Include taxes to add 10% to your desired margin to cover the expense.
  • Generate a buy order only to get a target buy price at the desired margin above the current buy order.
  • Generate a sell order only to get a target sell order at the desired margin below the current sell order.
  • Once your order has been fulfilled, remove it from your list of orders.
    • Do not refresh or leave this screen if you want to keep your list.
